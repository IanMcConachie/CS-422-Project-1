DATSR

Dux Amazing Time Series Repository





    
 The Problem: 

    
Time series analysis is a growing field of research and 
        is increasingly being applied to industry as well. 
    

    
However, despite interest in time series analysis, 
        there is no clear choice for a repository of this data. 
    

    
Other repositories exist, but lack certain key features 
        and/or have problems with the data they provide.
    

    

    
 Our Solution: 

    
We have built DATSR: an efficient, 
        user-friendly web-hosted repository 
        designed for time series data.
    

    
 
        DATSR allows users to not only upload and test their Time Series Models,
        but also allows them to upload their own datasets, both in Upload Your Files.
    

    

        Navigate to our Score Board page, to view the current High Score Models,
        or click on the Databases tab, to view our current available datasets.
